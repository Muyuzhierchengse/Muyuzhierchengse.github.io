I'm a fourth year undergraduate student from Department of Artificial Intelligence, [Communication University of China](https://www.cuc.edu.cn/). My research interest includes quantum computing, computation theory, machine learning and complex systems. 

In detail, I'm focusing on the computational limit of the quantum computing(Computational Complexity Theory) and the combination of the machine learning with quantum algorithms. What matters is, I'd prefer to figure out the basic theory than to achieve application, just like that I prefer the neural network itself than the new model which published to solve a exact problem.
